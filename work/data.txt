<strong>A.L.L.I.G.A.T.O.R. - Automated Learning and Linking for Intelligent Graph-based Association of Tabular Objects and Relationships</strong> constitutes an advanced platform that leverages machine learning algorithms to facilitate the process of entity linking within tabular datasets. 

This robust system interfaces with <a href='https://www.wikidata.org/'>Wikidata</a> to enrich tables by associating their elements with semantically linked entities. Our API provides a streamlined, programmable access to this capability, enabling users to integrate sophisticated entity resolution features into their applications.

To explore the API's functionalities, utilize the token: <code>alligator_demo_2023</code> during your requests.
